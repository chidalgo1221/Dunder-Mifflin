Overview:

Dunder Mifflin Inc. (stock symbol DMI) is a mid-cap regional paper- and office-supply distributor with an emphasis on servicing small-business clients. With a corporate office in New York City, Dunder Mifflin has branches in Albany, Utica, Scranton, Akron, Nashua, Buffalo, and Rochester.
